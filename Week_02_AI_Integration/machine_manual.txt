SECTION 1: SAFETY
- Always wear PPE (Personal Protective Equipment) including safety glasses and steel-toe boots.
- Lock-out/Tag-out (LOTO) procedures must be followed before opening any panel.
- Emergency Stop buttons are located on the main control panel and the rear maintenance door.

SECTION 2: HYDRAULIC SYSTEM
- The hydraulic pump operates at 120 PSI.
- If pressure drops below 100 PSI, check the relief valve on the manifold block.
- Fluid type: ISO VG 46 only. Change filter every 2000 hours.

SECTION 3: ELECTRICAL FAULTS
- Error E-404: Spindle Overload. Causes: Dull tool, excessive feed rate, or spindle bearing failure.
- Action for E-404: Reduce feed rate by 50%. If persists, check spindle amp draw.
- Error E-500: Communication Loss. Check the Ethernet cable between the HMI and the PLC.

SECTION 4: MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE
- Daily: Check coolant levels and way lube.
- Weekly: Clean chip conveyor and inspect spindle taper.
- Yearly: Replace backup battery in the servo drives.
